<h1>Navi - AI Travel Assistant</h1>
It is an AI based webapp that goes beyond the conventional applications of travel apps by compiling and providing the much needed information about the place being visited for travellers and tourists.
<h3>Problem Statement</h3>
Very often, tourists face a big challenge - not knowing what to do when in a new place. In a world very different from the one that they grew up in, it would be hard to meaningfully travel and make the most out of the stay.
<h3>Solution Overview</h3>
This app aims to bridge the gap between a tourist and her country of visit so that she can make the most out of the stay in the place.
<h3>Tech Stack Used</h3>
Visual Stuio was used to code the project.
Groq AI API was used to incorporate Artificial Intelligence and chatbot abilities into the project.
AI tools such as ChatGPT, Google Gemini, GitHub Copilot, and OpenAI Codex were used to improve the ideation process and development and debugging process.
